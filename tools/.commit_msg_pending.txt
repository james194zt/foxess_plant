Add native Solcast PV forecast with sun-aware polling and full-day charts.

Solcast is PV-only (Google Weather stays on StormSafe/overview). Rooftop
forecasts use PV tilt/azimuth, spread API calls across daylight from
sunrise until 1h before sunset, and require lat/lon matching Solcast site
listings (not HA coordinates). Charts show rest-of-day forecast with dashed
future segment; v0.9.7.
